Poem of the Dove Ceremony

Grandma, Adelaida, our great grandmother,
our grandmother, our mother,
jewel at the heart of our family,
sweetly we entered your life and
you gave us your lasting joy and love,
And with you we send our deepest love,
our boundless gratitude.
Now as you leave we stand together
to say good-bye,
And as you ascend to the angels
we hold your name in our hearts,
your memory shining bright.
Originally written on November 4, 2008

Notes: This poem was recited by me at my grandmother’s funeral which was held on Election Day 2008.
