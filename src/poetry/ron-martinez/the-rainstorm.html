The Rainstorm

Thunder crackles through the cloudy darkness,
while a misty wind fills the air.
The heavy rustic sky frees its burden,
a torrent of rain paints the scene.
There I witness from a bench,
the refreshing touch of raindrops.
The wet surroundings glisten,
Becoming dazzling spots of fresh.
Other people retreat indoors,
Avoiding the comfort in a soaked world.
Ambient noise of the rain beats hard,
The rhythm eases my daily burdens.
The searing rain treats me well.
Originally written on July 9, 2003
Most recently revised on August 1, 2010
