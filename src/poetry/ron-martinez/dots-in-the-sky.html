Dots in the Sky

So grand yet quietly distant
in their lasting glimmer
The reliable dots in the sky
shine for their admirers
While almost spelling your name
Your hand raises to catch one
should it decide to greet you
Don’t reach out for just any
only ones that stay a while
Don’t try for them all
that would be below unfair
There’s one you keep pointing at
the most brilliant to your eye
It’s looking for something to shine upon
and you fully offer your hand
But others within reach fall instead
Still, you must aim high…
Originally written on August 11, 2003
