All Tomorrow’s Parties

I get down
I get angry
I get bitter and so sad
But when a wink comes my way
I smile at distant lights
I get poor
I get lonely
I get regretful and so quiet
But when someone listens a little
I can easily rest at night
I get lost
I get dreaming
I’m always there and yet invisible
But if someone says they want me
Then there’s something to look forward to
I’ve been used
I’ve been forgotten
But I can be joyful and carefree
Just meet me at the party
And I’ll be with you in your laughter
Originally written on August 4, 2003
Most recently revised on August 1, 2010
