Acapella 2

In the cover of midnight,
there by the waterfront,
I couldn’t believe it was true,
that it was always you,
It’s the way that you do it,
the way that you say it,
It’s the every motion of each step in your walk,
It’s the way that you look at me,
talking so softly,
it’s the gleam of the moonbeams in your eyes…
Originally written on October 14, 2001
