Ambient Thoughts

in silence, i wandered, so in silence i pondered…
ambient dreams circling and swirling,
and a soft glow lulling me to stillness,
there i dreamt motionless,
imagining the soothing rhythms relieving me…
there i fell into trance,
and felt myself let go,
floating off into the light of the welcoming abyss,
i could stay in such an emotional inertia
for as long as i wished…
Originally written on November 5, 2001
Most recently revised on August 25, 2007
