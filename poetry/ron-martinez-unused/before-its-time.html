Before It’s Time

I stand on the brink of annihilation
My world is falling in on itself
toward one moment
where my chains of doubt and fear snap
and I am finally free to delivery my
one last act of redemption.
I stand at the edge of a cliff
There I prepare to welcome death
with open arms
I see only the horizon
and set off after it darkens.
Originally written on September 12, 2003
