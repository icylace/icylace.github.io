No Desire

to walk in the sunlight
where your shadow persists
if I am your savior
then why do you resist?
to feed my anger
walk in the clouds
evading every passion
in the world to atone
if I am the moonlight
and you feel alone
then why do you shudder
as I enter your home?
to be as the stranger
as your eyes in a way
and sit by the embers,
the dying light of day
Originally written on October 27, 2001
Most recently revised on January 13, 2004
