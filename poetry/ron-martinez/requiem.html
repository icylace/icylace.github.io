Requiem

In your loving memory,
I recite my passage of tears,
my reflection along your path,
I ask for forgiveness,
I should have been the one your eyes beheld last,
and in your loving memory,
I stand here, by the sunrise,
finally understanding why you awoke so early for all these years,
and then I come to think,
nothing looked so beautiful as your silhouette complementing the sky,
an image burned in my eyes,
for as long as I hold dear your loving memory…
Originally written on October 15, 2001
