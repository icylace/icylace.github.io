As It Is

As it is with new things
and new friends
many lessons are learned.
Perspective expands,
skills improve,
old ambitions rekindle.
My heart is where
it has always been.
In a place where
our past is forgiven.
Someday,
we’ll find each other.
And when I share what
I have found with you
I will see you
for the first time
in the way you
tried telling me
in the first place—
as my dearest friend
but not my destiny.
Originally written on August 4, 2010
Most recently revised on March 15, 2011
