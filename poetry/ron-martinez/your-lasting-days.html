Your Lasting Days

Though your heart is not now that passion
which in youthful years made you dream,
that which you have, you have.
An honest love,
purest in form and tempered by regret,
yet enduring in hope and endearing in desire.
’Til your final moments
you would spend all your remaining days
in search of your happiness.
To love.
To be loved.
To have.
And finally let go.
Originally written on July 25, 2003
Most recently revised on February 15, 2011
