Avenging Angel

A passionate indignation raises me,
and wrought with the sighs of anguish,
a seething vengeance becomes me.
Every facet of my being burns in atonement,
emanating the brilliance of an accusing light.
Fate would have me cast down a belated justice,
long forgotten under the shame of our forebears.
And when I have fallen,
my fury shall reap the world of my victimizers,
and there will be no innocence…
Originally written on December 6, 2001
Most recently revised on August 4, 2010
