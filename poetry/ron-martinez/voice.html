Voice

How well are you heard?
How do you speak out?
What words gain attention?
Where is your voice?
Is your tongue cut out?
Would you speak if no one listened?
Why wait forever?
To place blame for missed opportunity?
Do you prefer it this way?
Where do you go to scream?
Who really cares?
It is moot speculation
until your voice is found.
Originally written sometime between 2000 and 2002
Most recently revised on August 3, 2010
