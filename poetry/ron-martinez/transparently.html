Transparently

In this world I dwell
where am I to be seen?
If you ask someone next to me
they may shake their head.
Then you may wonder about that,
since I’m there by their side.
And when you tell them,
they turn baffled,
walking away,
dropping their trash
for me to pick up.
Originally written on August 7, 2001
Most recently revised on August 3, 2010
