Sunlit Waterfall

An embracing warm mist
invited me to stay
by a sunlit waterfall.
Once a barren cliff
bears now a roaring stream
ending in white splashes
against supine rocks.
Through this vertical deluge,
I heard the haunting voice
of a shimmering phantom
obscured behind the falling water.
I could not guess why she was there.
I only knew the beauty of her song.
The fellow lament
of the missing half
of my broken heart?
The siren’s pitch
that cracked it
in the first place?
A peer expressing truth
in this
world of deception?
Which it is
whether any
or all
or none
I cannot say.
But dare I wonder
I'll find out
soon enough.
