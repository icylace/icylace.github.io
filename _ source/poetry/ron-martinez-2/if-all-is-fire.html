If All Is Fire

Whispers
Fiery winds call out
Pulsing from a mortal frame
Sandstorms and embers
Cry out
Down and flickering
Terrifying sparks
Like ghosts on a weave
Brimming over the horizon
If all is fire
Phantoms become flame
Vapors
Stale riverbeds recoil slowly
Surging with dust
Grey death
Plumes proudly
Ashen pillows teeming with laughter
Vibrant memories
Glowing warmth and searing passion
Brighten the depths
Blazing hands
Aching from their burning caress
Leaving cinders and silence
If all is fire
Away the world burns
But
All is fire
At the rhythm of your name.
Originally written on February 2, 2011
