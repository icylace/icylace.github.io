World Progress

Through ages past and destinies awakened,
this world was born.
The fruit of time, the boon of ideals,
the embodiment of the struggle.
But how many of each is content with their own,
and how many more beneath dream of it.
Though our world has went forth in our eyes,
this world has only made a half-motion to a forward step.
Originally written on October 20, 2001
Most recently revised on October 21, 2001
