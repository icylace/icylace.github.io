On the Brink

Here I am at the edge of the cliffside of a mountain
that I spent many years struggling to climb.
Here I stand at the end of destiny,
leaning forward into the unknown.
My thoughts claim death,
my heart claims life.
Rebirth of the soul.
Rebirth of the light.
Still, this is the end I feel,
And how full of relief I feel,
to experience the death of fear,
the death of expectation,
the death of illusion,
the death of regret.
Originally written on March 31, 2005
Most recently revised on June 16, 2005
