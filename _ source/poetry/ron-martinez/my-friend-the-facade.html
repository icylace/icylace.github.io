My Friend the Facade

The apology in front of the grin,
the gloating over the win,
these things were said,
by my friend the Facade.
An exaggerated victory story,
with equally embellished glory,
these lies were spread,
by my good friend the Facade.
A patronizing stare with a smile,
A degrading glare all the while,
these looks were made,
by my close friend the Facade.
A million truths to backup the lie,
now I seem to know why,
these fallacies were made,
by my dear friend the Facade.
Originally written before 2000
