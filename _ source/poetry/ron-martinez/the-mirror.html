The Mirror

What has remained the same
when I see my friends
move on with their lives
is how I miss them.
The happiness they’ve achieved
is the same that I seek
but instead of envy
there’s only desire
to be with them.
My reflection
is not the face that I see
but the aura of my friends
whose laughter and tears shine brightly
when the sun hits the mirror.
If I never make it
to where they are
I wonder who I will become
but yet I know
we will reunite someday
if for no other reason
than I love them.
To cherish friends
is to embrace yourself.
To find old friends
is to find missing shards of your soul.
That is the secret after all.
Originally written on March 18, 2011
