Fate’s Reprise

I’ve heard the voice of an angel falling,
and felt the pulse of a primal calling,
and within this recurring nightly rapture,
I dreamt a dream I longed to capture,
an elusive image I’ve held to heart,
a secret setting in a secluded world,
with all the time I would never have,
to see the face that haunts my dreams,
to hear this utterance that justifies hope,
“I have always missed you.”
Then the sunrise begins,
and daybreak shatters the trance.
If only time would grant me pardon,
in fate’s reprise.
Originally written on April 5, 2001
Most recently revised on April 4, 2002
