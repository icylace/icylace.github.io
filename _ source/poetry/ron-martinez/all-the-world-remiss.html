All the World Remiss

Your eyes are stars, the wind carries your voice,
It’s such a peaceful night to think of you,
I’d bring you back if there was a choice,
You were the soothing stream within my view.
I now know what the rain can take and bring,
But your hand guides me throughout my solemness,
And, like a nightingale, at night I sing,
About your sunshine after pouring sadness.
This night we could have been in our rapture,
The very feeling that I ever miss,
In a moment which I yearn to capture,
When eyes dream, lips meet, and we kiss!
Those memories do haunt me, find my door,
Oh, I miss thee and here I love you more!
Originally written on February 14, 2000
