Been A While

I was minding my own business,
enjoying a drink in the cafe,
working hard as usual,
when I noticed a strange girl,
standing in line by the counter,
who was smiling at me.
I’m pretty sure it was me,
no one else here in the corner,
it’s not like I was bothered,
just maybe a little surprised.
I tried to ignore her,
I was focusing on my work,
but she kept smiling at me,
the corner of my eye couldn’t avoid it.
Finally,
I gave up and looked at her,
she was sipping her fresh coffee,
her smile was still mine.
I should at least return the smile,
and that’s exactly what I did.
She then nodded mysteriously
and walked out the door.
What a strange girl.
I watched her as she left.
The afternoon wind played with her hair.
She took another sip of her coffee.
She was probably just passing by.
As I wondered about her,
I wanted to go eat some ice cream.
It’s been a while
since I felt like that.
